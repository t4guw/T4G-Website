Leetcode

We are working to bring technical interview skills into the classroom by teaching CS students how to approach solving technical questions. We are writing an online course at stepik.org. Solutions are written in C++. We are using github for our project management and solution code. We communicate on discord and meet on zoom.
Participants: Professor Pisan, Isabel, Mitchell, Taylor, Bill, Sam , Jonathan , and Boyan
Category: CS Education

Games With a Purpose

Games can serve a purpose for the user and the creator by harnessing human ability in an entertaining way. Skies the limit. The last group designed a mutli-level game that taught users about the experience of PTSD and incorporated techniques that promote healing behaviors for people with PTSD.
Participants: Professor Pisan, Jay, Keziah, Marcela
Category: Game Design
